PERKS

Quick Revive

It will take you half the normal time to revive another player.

Location: All maps

Juggernog

It increases the players health allowing them to take more damage from enemies.

Location: All maps

Speed Cola

Speed Cola halves reload time and allows barriers to be built 60% faster

Location: All maps

Stamin-Up

Affects the player's overall mobility, as it increases movement speed.

Location: All maps, except Die Rise

Double Tap

Increases the player's fire rate by 33%.

Location: All maps

Mule Kick

Mule Kick grants the player a third weapon slot, so as to be able to carry a third gun

Location: All maps

Electric Cherry

Electric Cherry creates a lethal electric shockwave whenever they reload their weapon.

Only Mob of the Dead

Vulture Aid

Cause players standing inside it to be ignored by other zombies.

Location: Only Buried

Who's Who

Who's Who allows the player to take control of a doppleganger when they go down

Location: Only Die Rise

Tombstone

Tombstone Soda allows the player to kill themselves while down, and recover their equipment.

Location: TranZit
